A little about me

I am a dedicated 4th-year Computer Science and Engineering student at KIIT University, Bhubaneswar, with a passion for data analysis and machine learning. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in computer science principles, while my hands-on experiences have allowed me to apply these concepts in real-world scenarios. During my internship at RnPSoft Pvt Ltd, I developed a one-to-one video calling web app using the WebRTC framework, Socket.io technology, and React, showcasing my ability to handle complex software development projects. Additionally, I created a real-time chat application using React.Js, further enhancing my skills in data collection, cleaning, and database management. At Celebal Technologies, I served as a Data Analyst intern, where I utilized Power BI to create visually appealing reports that facilitated better business decision-making. This experience underscored the importance of data visualization in communicating insights effectively to stakeholders. My academic projects reflect my analytical prowess and technical expertise. I developed a Movie Recommender System using Sentiment Analysis, which involved extensive data analysis, statistical techniques, and data visualization to extract meaningful insights from large datasets. I also created a Food and Diet Recommender Web App, a Text Summarizer Web App, a 3D Bakery Shop using Three.js, and a Text Emotion Detection Web App, each project highlighting different aspects of my programming and analytical skills. Additionally, my Multiple Disease Prediction System demonstrated my ability to integrate machine learning models to predict various diseases, showcasing my proficiency in predictive analytics. I am certified in Exploratory Data Analysis for Machine Learning, demonstrating my commitment to continuous learning and professional development. My proficiency in SQL, Python, and data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI enables me to efficiently manage and analyze data, create compelling visualizations, and communicate findings effectively. As I continue to engage with industry trends and advancements, I am eager to contribute my skills and knowledge to a dynamic team, tackling complex business problems with data-driven solutions and innovative approaches.
